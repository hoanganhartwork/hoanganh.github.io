F - Camp 2021




Maybe you already know or you may not know that F-camp is a welcoming event for new students of FPT University. This special event is held once a year by the students of the previous course and is only intended to welcome new students who have joined the FPT family. For this event to take place, it takes a lot of work and a lot of time, although everyone had to pour a lot of sweat, tears, even blood, but just need a laugh. everyone is still trying to overcome difficulties to prepare a memorable event in the student life of the freshman students.
